Nixu Corporation

Headcount: 123 persons

Products & Industry: Cybersecurity services for enterprises

Revenue: 12 millions

Key geographies: Northern Europe, China, USA

WHAT IS OUR PURPOSE?

We keep the digital society running.When companies and public sector are digitalizing their services, the continuity and secure access to those services demands functioning, well managed cybersecurity. We are the partner behind the scenes making sure the digital society stays usable.

WHAT DO WE AIM FOR BY 2020?

We want to be the number one choice for cybersecurity services for companies headquartered in Northern Europe. At the same time we want to be the best workplace for security professionals.

WHAT DO WE NEED HELP WITH?

We need help in developing our business model from consultancy to services business where we are a horizontal partner providing cybersecurity across client organization. This requires multitude of skills and talents from technical capability to business understanding.

WHY SHOULD WE TEAM UP?

We are a company on an ambitious growth mission. We have modern, non-hierarchical organization, where success is based on your ability to grow, influence and execute. We really want to be the best team around and are putting lot of focus to our employees.
